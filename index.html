applicable Theory CS Group

lead by Girish Varma at IIIT Hyderbad focusses on innovative uses of Theoretical Computer Science methods to applied problems from Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Transporation, Medical and Natural Science Domains. The group brings together members with pure theory interests as well as systems/machine learning expertise to look at applied problems from a rigorous theoretical perspective. We conduct courses and reading groups which explore these aspects together.
